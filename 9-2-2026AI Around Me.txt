#AI Around Me

1. 📱 Google Maps
Uses ML to analyze traffic data, past routes, and user behavior to predict the fastest route and estimated arrival time.

2. 🎬 Netflix
Recommends movies using ML models (like collaborative filtering) that learn from your watch history and similar users.

3. 📧 Gmail
Spam detection uses classification models trained on labeled emails to identify spam vs important messages.

4. 🛒 Amazon
Suggests products using recommendation systems based on your browsing, purchases, and similar customer behavior.

5. 🎤 Google Assistant
Uses speech recognition (deep learning) to convert voice to text and NLP models to understand and respond.

6. 📸 Instagram
Face detection, filters, and feed ranking use computer vision and ML models to personalize content.

7. ⌨️ Gboard
Predictive text uses language models trained on large text data to suggest next words while typing.

8. 🎵 Spotify
Recommends songs using ML by analyzing listening habits and patterns of similar users.

9. 💳 Fraud Detection Systems
ML models detect unusual transaction patterns and flag suspicious activities in real time.

10. 🛍️ Flipkart
Uses ML for search ranking and personalized recommendations based on user clicks and purchase history
