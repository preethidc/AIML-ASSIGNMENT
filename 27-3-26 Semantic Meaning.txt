1.Car – Automobile  *Very High
Both words refer to the same type of vehicle. “Automobile” is a formal term for “car.”

2.Teacher – Educator *High
Both describe a person who teaches or provides education.

3.Happy – Joyful  *High
These words express similar positive emotions and feelings.

4.Dog – Puppy *Medium
A puppy is a young dog, so they are related but not exactly identical.

5.Book – Pencil *Low
Both are used in education, but their meanings are different and unrelated semantically.
