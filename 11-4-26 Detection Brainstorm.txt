Security Surveillance System Using Object Detection

Problem:
Traditional CCTV cameras only record videos and require humans to monitor them continuously. Suspicious activities may be missed due to human error.

Proposed Solution:
Develop an AI-powered surveillance system that can automatically detect suspicious objects or unauthorized persons in real time.

How the System Works:
*CCTV camera captures live video.
*AI model analyzes each video frame.

The system detects:
*Unknown persons
*Weapons or suspiciou*s objects
*Unauthorized entry
*Abandoned bags/items.

If suspicious activity is detected:
*Alarm is triggered
*Notification is sent to security staff
*Screenshot/video is saved automatically.
